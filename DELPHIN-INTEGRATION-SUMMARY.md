**Scale**: 40,000 feet (12 km) - extraordinarily tall  
**Function**: Generates all power for city AND maintains consciousness frequency  
**Technology**: Crystal structure with resonance properties

### 3. Resonant Frequency System
**Mechanism**: Base frequency generated by spire, maintained throughout city  
**Effect**: Keeps inhabitants "consciously connected"  
**Result**: Unity consciousness experienced in daily life

**How**: Learning by one individual transfers to all in the field  
**Method**: Elevated knowledge form imprints on collective consciousness  
**Impact**: No traditional education needed for information transmission
