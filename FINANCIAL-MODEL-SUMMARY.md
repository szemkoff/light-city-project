## Risk Factors

1. **Permitting delays**: 12-24 month timeline (mitigated with pre-app meetings, experienced consultants)
2. **Construction cost escalation**: 20-30% contingency included
3. **Resident recruitment**: Pre-sales de-risk demand
4. **Operating losses**: 12-18 month reserve fund
5. **Community conflict**: Governance framework, conflict resolution process
