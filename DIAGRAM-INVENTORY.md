## üé® Visual Style Guide

All custom SVG diagrams follow a consistent design language:

**Colors:**
- Primary: `#667eea` (Blue-purple)
- Secondary: `#764ba2` (Deep purple)
- Accent: `#f093fb` (Pink-purple)
- Light: `#a0d8ff` (Sky blue)
- Gold: `#feca57` (Golden yellow)
- White: `#ffffff` (Highlights)

**Typography:**
- Font: Arial, sans-serif
- Title size: 18-24px
- Body text: 10-14px
- Always include descriptive labels

**Layout:**
- Consistent padding and margins
- Information boxes with rounded corners
- Semi-transparent overlays (opacity: 0.9-0.95)
- Clear visual hierarchy
