# Code of Conduct

Light City is a sacred collaborative space. We commit to:

## Our Pledge
- Treat all participants with kindness, respect, and dignity
- Welcome diverse perspectives and traditions
- Assume positive intent and seek understanding
- Create psychologically and spiritually safe spaces

## Our Standards
- Use compassionate language; avoid personal attacks
- Offer critique that is specific and actionable
- Honor consent and privacy, especially in spiritual sharing
- No harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary behavior

## Reporting & Enforcement
- Report concerns to maintainers via GitHub Issues or email (to be added)
- Maintainers may take appropriate action: guidance, warnings, or removal
