- **Sacred Gold**: #FFD700 - Accents, CTAs, highlights

**Accent Colors**:
- **Sky Blue**: #A0D8FF - Trust, water, clarity
- **Emerald Green**: #90EE90 - Nature, growth, sustainability
- **Coral Pink**: #F093FB - Warmth, community, heart

**Semantic Colors**:
- **Success**: #32CD32 (Green) - Completed, proven
- **Warning**: #FFD700 (Gold) - Important, attention
- **Info**: #4169E1 (Blue) - Information, research
- **Danger**: #DC143C (Red) - Critical, risk

#### Typography
**Headings**:
- Font: Inter, Helvetica Neue, sans-serif
- H1: 48px, bold, letter-spacing: -0.02em
- H2: 36px, semibold
- H3: 28px, semibold
- H4: 22px, medium

**Body**:
- Font: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- Size: 16px (desktop), 14px (mobile)
- Line height: 1.6
- Color: #333333 (dark gray, not pure black)

**Special**:
- Code: JetBrains Mono, Courier New, monospace
- Quotes: Georgia, serif (italic)

#### Sacred Geometry Elements
- **Flower of Life**: Background watermark (subtle, 5% opacity)
- **Golden Ratio**: Layout proportions (1.618:1)
- **Circular Motifs**: Buttons, avatars, icons
- **Hexagonal Grid**: Card layouts, image galleries
- **Spiral Patterns**: Loading animations, transitions

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over Complexity**: Every element serves a purpose
2. **Whitespace is Sacred**: Generous spacing, breathable layouts
3. **Progressive Disclosure**: Show essentials first, details on demand
4. **Biophilic Design**: Nature-inspired patterns, organic shapes
5. **Accessibility First**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance minimum

   - Answer: Illiquid for 5-7 years. Secondary market possible after Year 3 with founder approval.

   - Answer: Right of first refusal to other investors or founder. Discounted valuation if before Year 5.

### Important Context for AI Creators

#### **Scale Clarification (CRITICAL)**
- **Delphin** (Off-World Reference): 40,000 ft spire, 600-1,200 ft domes, 10,000 people per dome
- **Light City Earth** (Buildable): 60-300m spire, 30-90m domes, 80-400 people per dome
- **Always separate these clearly** - Delphin is inspirational, not literal for Earth

#### **Energy System (CRITICAL)**
- **Physical Energy**: Solar PV + batteries provide ALL essential power (proven, TRL 8-9)
- **Subtle Energy**: Acoustic resonance, consciousness research (experimental, TRL 1-3)
- **Never claim**: "Consciousness generates electrical power" or "spire powers the city"
- **Always clarify**: "Spire generates acoustic resonance; microgrid provides electrical power"

#### **Financial Model (CRITICAL)**
- **Phase 1 Target**: 200 residents (150-300 range)
- **Unit Mix**: 50 tiny homes, 80 studios, 50 1-BR, 20 2-BR = 200 units
- **Cross-Subsidy**: Tiny homes are loss leaders, subsidized by larger units + retreat revenue
- **Retreat Revenue**: Essential ($400K-$900K/year), not optional
- **Break-Even**: 180 residents + 600-800 retreat guests/year

#### **Governance (CRITICAL)**
- **Stages 1-4**: Founder-led (development agility)
- **Stages 5-6**: Community Council (operational democracy)
- **Investor Protection**: Board observer rights, financial reporting, major decision approval
- **Founder Veto**: Limited to 5 core principles only

#### **Timeline (CRITICAL)**
- **Q4 2025**: Complete Stage 1 (Foundation) - 85% done as of Nov 14
- **Q2 2026**: Complete Stage 2 (Design)
- **Q4 2026**: Complete Stage 3 (Digital)
- **Q4 2027**: Complete Stage 4 (Community)
