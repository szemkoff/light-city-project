### Key Features Visible

1. **Central Spire**
   - Extraordinarily tall (40,000 feet according to source)
   - Crystalline/faceted appearance
   - Blue-white coloring
   - Tapers to point at top
   - Luminous quality

2. **City Layout**
   - Perfectly circular
   - Concentric rings of development
   - Radial pathways from center to edge
   - Geometric precision in street grid
   - Dense middle ring with buildings
   - Open plaza area around central spire

3. **Communospheres**
   - Large dome structures
   - Positioned at city perimeter
   - Multiple spheres (at least 6 visible)
   - Evenly spaced around circle
   - Appear metallic or crystalline
   - Semi-transparent quality

4. **Natural Integration**
   - Surrounded by forest/green landscape
   - Clear boundary between city and nature
   - City appears to float or be elevated
   - Harmonious integration not domination

### Design Elements to Incorporate

**From these images, Light City should include:**

- Circular city layout with central point
- Extraordinarily tall central spire
- Concentric ring organization
- Radial pathway system
- Large spherical structures at perimeter
- Dense middle zone with varied buildings
- Open central plaza
- Integration with surrounding nature
- Crystalline/luminous aesthetic
- Geometric precision in layout
- Semi-transparent architectural quality

### Color Palette Observed

- **Spire**: Blue-white, crystalline, luminous
- **Communospheres**: Metallic/crystalline, blue-gray tones
- **Buildings**: Dark (possibly dark crystal or stone)
- **Landscape**: Natural greens, earth tones
- **Overall**: Cool color temperature, ethereal quality
- **Light**: Emanating from structures, not external
